This quilt I made for my newly born niece. It was the first quilt I had made in quite a while, since I had moved to a new house I now had the space to start quilting again. I did not have many fabrics, but my partner bought me some pink and blue fabrics from Lidl and I then added some yellow fabric to them to brighten it up. Because I had not quilted for some time, I decided to do a sampler type quilt to get back into making lots of different patchwork blocks. Lots of my old favourite blocks, including churn dash and the old windmill. For the white background of the blocks, I used an old sheet, I wouldn\u2019t recommend this as it was quite thin, and I wonder if it will stand the test of time.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
