This quilt I made for a young girl who loves unicorns. The unicorn is a pattern by Elizabeth Hartman (https:\/\/elizabethhartman.com\/<\/a>) and I added some star of hope 6 inch blocks and some squares of unicorn fabric to make it bigger along with a nice border of rainbow stary fabric. The finished size is around a metre square. It’s not possible to tell from the photo but the horn is done with glittery fabric! I found a lovely line of unicorn fabric called Believe by Kim Schaefer and found fabrics from my stash and from the shop to go with them. The photo below shows the star Believe fabric layed out with the fabrics I chose to do the unicorn mane in. The rainbow is fairly pastely and an interesting combination of colours, it was fun to find fabrics to go with it.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
