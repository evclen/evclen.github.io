I have always admired these quilts; I love log cabins and to me the pineapple quilt just look like an extreme version of the log cabin. I knew it would take quite a while, it took me a whole year in the end, but since it was covid I had the time. Furthermore, I did not have much fabric back then as I was just getting back into quilting, however I was able to scrounge a lot of scraps from my mother.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

To plan this quilt, I printed off a line drawing of the pineapple pattern with the navy-blue sections filled in and coloured in some ideas I had for colour placements. I ended up choosing a design with all the blocks being the same with the greens and blues opposite and the fabrics getting darker and darker as they got further from the centre. I laid out all my fabrics in 5 green piles and 5 blue piles, with each pile a different shade.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
