When I was a baby, I had a Japanese folded patchwork “circles into squares” quilt that my mother made me, it was all made of blue and green batik fabrics as is beautiful. I thought I would make my own, as I wanted a long project and had the perfect fabric for it. I had spotted this rainbow Alison Glass collection called “Thicket” and asked my family if they might buy me the fabric bundle for my birthday so that I could make this quilt. To go with it, we picked out a bluey teal linen texture fabric that would act as the background fabric.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
