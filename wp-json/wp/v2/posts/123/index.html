I always fancied making a space quilt, I loved space when I was a child and when my friends gave birth to their 4th son I thought this would be the prefect opportunity. Surely 4 boys would love a space quilt, so a year later for his Christening it was finished.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

I wanted all 8 planets to feature in the quilt, sized to scale with each other and also the sun. As Mercury is the smallest, I set that to be around an inch or two and scaled the rest of the planets from that, using either a compass to get the curvature or a long piece of string with a pencil on the end. I also featured three rockets, for which I designed the block for myself and some fabric which had some facts about each planet which I appliqu\u00e9d on close to each planet. The planets were appliqu\u00e9d on using either bond-a-web or spray adhesive and then a zigzag stitch.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
